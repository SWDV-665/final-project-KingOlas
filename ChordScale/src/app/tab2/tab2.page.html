Info / How-To Info / How-To Founder Bio Olas Ortwein (1991 - ) Growing up and performing as a professional musician, Olas decided after the pandemic to search for more stability in order to continue his passion for music. He found a deeper appreciation for music software during the beginning of the pandemic, and decided to pursue a new career in development.

By December 2021, Olas will have completed his Master's of Science in Software Development from Maryville University, St. Louis.
